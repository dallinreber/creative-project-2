Tips and Tricks

Hello! I hope you are doing well. This page is all about helping people find ways to come back to the anxiety, stress, and fear that plagues our world today. I’m no doctor, I’m just sharing some of the things that I have found to help me stay grounded and maintain a healthy and happy outlook on my life and my future.

To anyone who asks me, I always say the most important things are first a healthy diet, healthy sleep schedule, and a healthy hobby. Some form of exercise, you know? If we are trying to maintain these three things, we aren’t giving ourselves a fighting chance.

My next word of advice would be to practice meditation and mindfulness. Set a side a part of your home or living space where you can quiet down, and make sure it is away from any distractions. Turn your phone off, or set it in a different room. If you were religious, praying can be an awesome way to meditate or practice mindfulness. Yoga, breathing exercises, or even meditation apps can also be very beneficial. This can really help with living in the present, which is key to finding hope and peace.
